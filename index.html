Our Story

Our director, Mr.Bipin M. Sheth started his business when he was just 18 years old. His leadership qualities and never give up attitude has lead him through all the hurdles and helped him emerge as a winner in true sense. With being successful in becoming a good human being he is also a successful businessman looking out for every opportunity.

He has been the team leader when helping people of Uttarakhand for flood relief work. Also, he has lead a team to help the people of Nepal in their times of distress. That is when he founded the Architectural consultation wing in his company which served the needy people of Nepal free of cost, headed by Jinali Sheth. Making huge contributions to the society in cash and kind makes him more happy than making business deals. He has been a crucial part of leading the team animal welfare.




His passionate perfectionism, enthusiastic commitment, piercing dedication and vision, is what lies behind the success of his ventures. Mr.Bipinkumar earns not only for his family but also for the people in need. He has been awarded by the State government for this significant contribution during Maharashtra drought.

With being successful in becoming a good human being he is also a successful businessman looking out for every opportunity. He believes a businessman should never restrict himself to a particular field and therefore has started ventures in the field of architecture and information technology.
